    About

    A Telegram Mini App for Hermes secrets.

    Secrets you save here are written to ~/.hermes/.env on the Hermes host, so the agent can use them on the next request — no Telegram round-trip, no LLM in the path.

    Auth is handled by validating the Telegram Mini App init data against the bot token. Anyone else gets a 403.
